# Upcoming Webinar: Neutral Governance for the Gardener Project

Gardener, one of our projects at the [NeoNephos Foundation](https://neonephos.org/), hosts a public webinar on **September 24th**  at 3:00 pm CEST / 6:30 pm IST. In this webinar you will learn how Gardener aligns with NeoNephos’ goals, how it works under the hood, and why it matters for the future of cloud infrastructure in Europe and beyond. **Marc Vornetran**, Software Engineer, and **Niclas Moldenhauer**, Engineering Manager, both at SAP, will also talk about Gardener’s features, recent developments, and give live demos.

-   **Speakers**: Marc Vornetran, Software Engineer and Niclas Moldenhauer, Engineering Manager, SAP
-   **Host**: SAP Open Source Program Office

**Registration (external):** [link to registration](https://events.sap.com/ospo-webinar-series/en_us/home.html)
