# Join

We are a community of cloud technology enthusiasts and welcome new members at any scale.

For the exact conditions and benefits available to members read through [our charter](https://cdn.platform.linuxfoundation.org/agreements/neonephos-foundation.pdf?__hstc=81619592.11739e1f4ca03192f4e918409f725053.1748961872693.1753863796744.1753964512976.20&__hssc=81619592.3.1753964512976&__hsfp=2164450451).

## Membership Tiers

We have three membership tiers available - Premier, General and Associate memberships.

| Premier | General  | Associate  |
|---|---|---|
| [50k € p.a. - 100k € p.a.](#premier-member-fee-scale)  | [5k € p.a. - 50k € p.a.](#general-member-fee-scale)  | free  |
| Voting Governing Board Seat  | [Shared Voting Governing Board Seat](#shared-general-governing-board-representative) | Observer Status in Governing Board  |
| Voting Technical Advisory Council Seat  | Observer Status in Technical Advisory Council | Observer Status in Technical Advisory Council   |

## Premier Member Fee Scale

| Consolidated Employees | Fee  |
|---|---|
|up to 200|75k € p.a.|
|more than 200|100k € p.a.|

## General Member Fee Scale

| Consolidated Employees | Fee  |
|---|---|
| under 100| 5k € p.a.|
| up to 499| 10k € p.a.|
| up to 4999| 30k € p.a.|
| more than 5000| 50k € p.a.|

## Shared General Governing Board Representative

One voting representative for every 5 General Members.
