# Fraunhofer ISST joins NeoNephos as an Associate Member

We are excited to announce the addition of one additional Associate Member, the [Fraunhofer Institute for Software and Systems Engineering (ISST)](https://www.isst.fraunhofer.de/en.html), to our foundation! 
The Fraunhofer Institute for Software and Systems Engineering ISST conducts applied research on smart data ecosystems and contributes to the development of sovereign, interoperable, and sustainable software for the digital economy. More generally, the Fraunhofer Society is a storied name in applied research and has been associated with many breakthrough projects such as the [mp3 compression algorithm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MP3). NeoNephos, notably hosting the development of an interoperable cloud reference architecture, greatly appriciates the input from "research veterans" who are used to advancing the frontier in unchartered terrain.

Interested in shaping the future of cloud-native sovereignty by joining our foundation? NeoNephos offers different membership tiers which accommodate companies and non-profit organizations of all sizes. See here to learn more about the process!
