# NeoNephos Guest Article in Techzine

According to the [mission statement in our charter](https://cdn.platform.linuxfoundation.org/agreements/neonephos-foundation.pdf?__hstc=81619592.11739e1f4ca03192f4e918409f725053.1748961872693.1755169332458.1755604645613.29&__hssc=81619592.2.1755604645613&__hsfp=1879682750), NeoNephos advances the goals of the IPCEI CIS.
Therefore, all our projects are selected with the overarching goal of establishing an interoperable cloud native stack.
In [our guest commentary in Techzine magazine](https://www.techzine.eu/experts/devops/139288/advancing-europes-public-agenda-through-open-source-software-foundations/) we briefly explain how open source foundations may serve to address shortcomings in the digital economy through industry collaboration and community participation.
