# New General Members join NeoNeoNephos

**Open Source Summit, Amsterdam, NL – August 25, 2025**  – The NeoNephos Foundation, which supports projects that align with the strategic objectives of the EU’s IPCEI-CIS, is excited to announce the addition of four new General Members.

**New General Members**

-   [Liquid Reply](https://www.reply.com/liquid-reply/en)
-   [Ape Factory](https://www.apefactory.com/en)
-   [Nearby Computing](https://www.nearbycomputing.com/)
-   [Kubermatic](https://www.kubermatic.com/)

The addition of these partners highlights NeoNephos’ commitment to inviting the industry at large in shaping the future of sovereign cloud-native technology collaboratively, all under the umbrella of the Linux Foundation Europe.

**About NeoNephos**  
NeoNephos, part of Linux Foundation Europe, provides a sustainable, shared development model for projects of the cloud-edge continuum and offers collaborative potential for higher adoption. By being open to hosting projects from other industry players and the community at large, the foundation provides a nurturing environment for any project sharing the goal of a sovereign, open, enterprise-hardened, multi provider cloud-to-edge continuum.  
Contributions to NeoNephos can be conducted in a variety of ways: by  [participating in projects](https://github.com/neonephos), [donating entire projects](https://neonephos.org/projects) or [becoming a member](https://neonephos.org/join/) (if you are an organization).

Follow along on the journey by [subscribing to the newsletter](https://neonephos.org/).
